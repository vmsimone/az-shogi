Welcome to the US Shogi Association!

About

While not the most popular game in the US, this exciting, traditional strategy game from Japan has had fans from the States since at least 1999! We wanted to bring some of that excitement into the modern day, make it easier for experienced players to find each other, and offer new and potential players an easy way to find shogi clubs and events in their local area.
