  • Format:
    • The Preliminary tournament and Individual tournament will both be Swiss Style, where the players with the most wins (against the highest ranked opponents) will rank the highest. The Semi-Finals will be single-elimination and the Semi-Finals winners will advance to the Finals, where the winners of each class will be crowned Champion.
    • For all rounds through the semi-finals, players will have 10 minutes of thinking time and 40 seconds of byouyomi. The Finals match will have 20 minutes of thinking time and 40 seconds of byouyomi.
    • For the team tournament, players will be organized by club and face-off round-robin style. Each will have 12 minutes ("kiremake", no byouyomi). A "Pro team" will also compete (with handicaps) and count towards the end result.
