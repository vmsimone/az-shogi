Shogi Streamers

In the age of the Internet, streaming games live and uploading recordings has become a popular hobby, and shogi is one such game! Find your new favorite shogi streamer below!

  • LilyLionmane

    A young but skilled player, Lily is a VTuber who regularly takes on multiple opponents simultaneously, reviews user submitted games, and competes in online tournaments. She dresses as a fairy, but she plays like an oni.

  • Shogi Dad (O Hi There)

    O Hi There, the shogi-playing dad sometimes known as Shogi Dad, is a familiar, friendly face to many US Shogi players. He regular streams speed-games he plays on ShogiWars. You'll laugh, you'll cry, you'll be sure to have a good time.
